Our Ecosystem

Key partners & stakeholders

CreativeLink is built in partnership with — and accountable to — the organizations and institutions that shape Kigali's creative economy.

Government

Rwanda Development Board (RDB)

Endorsement, SME funding referrals, and alignment with Rwanda's national digital transformation strategy.

Tech Ecosystem

kLab Innovation Hub

Platform development support, digital mentorship network, and connections to Rwanda's tech startup community.

Education

IPRC Kigali / Local Schools

Student pipeline, classroom integration, and access to the creative vocational training ecosystem.

Community

Nyamirambo Women's Cooperatives

Craft market access, community trust networks, and outreach channels to female creative entrepreneurs.

Development

GIZ / USAID Partners

NGO cohort funding, international development alignment, and impact reporting validation frameworks.

Telecom

MTN Rwanda / Airtel

Mobile money API integration, affordable data bundle partnerships, and SMS fallback notifications.
