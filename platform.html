The Platform

CreativeLink
Platform

Mobile-first. Bilingual. Community-rooted. Four integrated pillars that take a creative youth from raw talent to sustainable income.

📱 Mobile-first 🇷🇼 Kinyarwanda 🇫🇷 French 🆓 Free tier 🏅 Digital certificates
01

Skills Training

Bite-sized learning modules covering tailoring, music production, graphic design, photography, and crafts — structured into tracks and available in both Kinyarwanda and French.

Each module is designed for low-bandwidth mobile connections and takes 10–20 minutes to complete. Progress is tracked and displayed in the learner's dashboard.

5 skill tracks: tailoring, music, design, photography, crafts
Full Kinyarwanda and French content — no English required
Designed for low-bandwidth smartphones
Progress badges and completion tracking
Digital certificates issued on completion (3,000 RWF)

02

Mentor Matching

Browse verified local creative professionals — photographers, graphic designers, tailors, musicians — and book direct sessions. No referral needed. No gate-keepers.

Mentors post their availability, specialty, and portfolio. Youth message them directly, book sessions, and leave reviews. Year 1 target: 10 active mentors, 200 connections.

Verified local professional mentor network
Direct messaging and session booking
Mentor profiles with portfolio and specialization
Review and rating system for quality assurance
Mentor stipends introduced in Year 2

03

Portfolio Showcase

Upload photos and videos of your work. Get a shareable profile link. Be discovered by event organizers, NGOs, businesses, and clients across Kigali — without leaving Nyamirambo.

Free users can post up to 5 portfolio items. Premium users get unlimited uploads, priority placement in search results, and featured placement on the home feed.

Public profile with shareable link
Photo and video portfolio upload
Searchable by skill type and location
Visible to NGOs, businesses and event organizers
Premium: unlimited uploads + priority placement
🎨

04

Gig Marketplace

Businesses and event organizers post creative jobs. Youth browse, apply, get hired. Payments flow through the platform. CreativeLink takes a 5% commission — only when youth earn. Zero cost until success.

Year 1 target: 60 gigs posted by businesses, 150 completed by youth, generating RWF 10 million in collective user earnings.

Businesses post paid creative gigs
Youth apply and get hired directly
Secure payment through the platform
5% commission only on successful gigs
Free users: 2 applications/month. Premium: unlimited
💼

Technology

Built to scale

CreativeLink is built on an open-source stack that minimizes licensing costs and prevents vendor lock-in. Once the content is created, scaling to 10× more users costs near zero.

Cloud hosting scales automatically with user growth. Low-data-mode ensures the platform remains fast even on Rwanda's rural mobile networks.
