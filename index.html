Our Mission

A Think Tank for Community Change

"To tackle the unemployment rate in the Nyamirambo community in Kigali in order to improve the standards of living."

We believe every young person in Nyamirambo holds untapped creative potential. Our mission is to build the systems, tools, and networks that transform that potential into real economic opportunity — through arts, design, and culture.


Based at Nyamirambo Arts House, we work directly with the community, conducting field research, stakeholder interviews, and co-designing solutions with the youth we serve.

The Problem

A Gap Too Large to Ignore

Despite Rwanda's notable progress in job creation, a significant gap remains — and in communities like Nyamirambo, the barriers run deep.

15.4%

Unemployment rate in Nyamirambo — youth lack access to practical education, business skills, startup capital, and viable markets.

125,794

Jobs not created between 2017–2023 against Rwanda's national NST2 target of 1.5 million jobs.

40%

Of creative youth lose potential clients due to having no online presence or digital showcase for their work.

Our Central Question

"How can creativity be utilized to increase sustainable jobs by 80% in Nyamirambo by 2030 to increase people's standards of living?"

Our Solution

A Platform That Bridges the Gap

We are developing a web-based platform that teaches skills, connects youth with mentors, and opens doors to real job opportunities in the creative industry — rooted in Nyamirambo, built for Rwanda.

📚

Skills Training

Bite-sized, bilingual (Kinyarwanda + French) training modules covering practical creative skills. Mobile-first, low-bandwidth, built for the Nyamirambo reality.

🤝

Mentor Matching

Connecting youth with local creative professionals for one-on-one guidance. Filtered by skill, distance, and availability — community-centered mentorship.

🖼️

Talent Showcase

A digital portfolio system for youth to display their creative work to 500+ businesses browsing daily. Visibility beyond the street corner.

💼

Gig Marketplace

Real paid opportunities from Kigali Events, Nyamirambo Women Center, and Radio Rwanda. All payments via M-Pesa. 5% commission on successful gigs only.

"Bridging the opportunity gap for Nyamirambo's creative youth by linking skills development, mentorship, and market access to sustainable artistic employment."

Reach Strategy

Social media (Facebook, TikTok, Snapchat) via success stories and creative content to attract youth and sponsors.

Community Partners

Partnerships with schools and community organizations to introduce the platform directly to youth.

Implementation

Delivered by GoalDrivenElabers with support of creative professionals from Nyamirambo's creative industry.
