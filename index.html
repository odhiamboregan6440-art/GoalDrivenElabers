What We Built

Four pillars.
One platform.

CreativeLink integrates skills training, mentorship, portfolio showcase, and a gig marketplace into one mobile-first, bilingual platform — built for and by the Nyamirambo community.

See the Full Platform →
01

Skills Training

Bite-sized modules in Kinyarwanda & French — tailoring, music, design, photography, crafts.

02

Mentor Matching

Connect with verified local creative professionals. Direct messaging and session booking.

03

Portfolio Showcase

Upload your work. Get discovered by clients, NGOs and event organizers across Kigali.

04

Gig Marketplace

Browse and apply for paid creative jobs. No middleman. 5% commission only on success.

Our Journey

5 Challenges, 1 Mission

Follow GoalDriven Elabers through every stage — from introducing our vision in Nyamirambo to officially launching CreativeLink in the community.

Challenge 1

Introduction

We introduce GoalDriven Elabers, our vision for the platform, and the vibrant creative community of Nyamirambo that inspired it all.

Challenge 2

Discover Africa

Celebrating the unrecognized stories, victories, and creative achievements happening across Africa that the world rarely hears about.

Challenge 3

Help Lab

Community giveback in action — our team visited the most vulnerable members of Nyamirambo to show love and solidarity.

Challenge 4

Stakeholder Research

We went to the field — interviewing artists, designers, and cultural leaders including the Nyamirambo Women Center to understand the real challenges.

Challenge 5 · Final 🏆

Launching Our Mission

After deep community research, we officially launch CreativeLink in Nyamirambo — turning creative talent into sustainable careers.
