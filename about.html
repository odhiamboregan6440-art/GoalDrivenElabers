Our Story

Born from observation

We didn't build CreativeLink in a classroom. We built it in Nyamirambo — talking to tailors who lose 40% of their profit to borrowed equipment, musicians who cannot find clients outside their street, and graphic designers with professional-grade phones who have never been paid for digital work.

Five interviews became a movement. Our empathy mapping, stakeholder analysis, and PESTLE research confirmed what we had seen with our own eyes: this community is overflowing with talent and starved of systems.

CreativeLink is our answer. Not a generic platform repurposed for Rwanda — a platform built from scratch, in Kinyarwanda and French, for the specific reality of Nyamirambo's creative economy.

Our values

🌍

Community First

Every decision is tested against one question: does this serve the youth of Nyamirambo? Not investors, not metrics — people first.

🗣️

In Your Language

Kinyarwanda and French are not afterthoughts. They are the foundation. A platform that excludes you by language is not a platform for you.

📊

Evidence-Driven

Every feature, every price point, every partnership is grounded in research — interviews, data, and lived experience from inside the community.

🔗

Sustainable by Design

CreativeLink is built to outlast any grant. The business model generates revenue from day one so the community doesn't depend on charity.

📱

Mobile & Accessible

Designed for low-bandwidth smartphones. No desktop required. No English required. Just your skills and your phone.

🏆

Youth-Led

GoalDriven Elabers are students — but we are building something real. This is not a class project. This is a business that will run after we graduate.
